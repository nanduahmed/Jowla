Jawla Tracker Privacy Policy

    We don't store your data, period.

    • Google auhentication is required to access the Jawla list from your account and display it in the app. No data is transmitted to any other database
    • Jawla Tracker will only use access to read, write, modify, or control Google Sheets to provide a door-knocking web client that allows users to manage and keep track of contacts and previous visits and will not transfer this Sheets data to others.
    • The App will not use the Google account and data for serving advertisements
    • The App will not allow humans to read this data unless we have your affirmative agreement for specific messages, doing so is necessary for security purposes such as investigating abuse, to comply with applicable law, or for the App's internal operations and even then only when the data have been aggregated and anonymized.
    • Google Analytics is enabled for the only purpose of monitoring the traffic on the app
